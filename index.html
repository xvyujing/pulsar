X-ray counterparts of ATNF pulsars

The dataset represents the largest sample of X-ray counterparts of RPPs ever compiled, including 98 normal pulsars (NPs) and 133 millisecond pulsars (MSPs). This catalogue is constructed by correlating ATNF pulsar catalogue v2.3.0 with XMM-Newton Serendipitous Source Catalog (4XMM-DR13 Version) and Chandra Source Catalog Release 2.0 (CSC 2.0).
